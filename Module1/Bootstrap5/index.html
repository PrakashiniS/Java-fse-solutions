Frequently Asked Questions

You can register for events in several ways:

  • Click the "Register Now" button on any event card
  • Visit our registration page
  • Contact our event coordinators directly

Ticket prices vary by event type and package. Generally, they include:

  • Access to all main event activities
  • Event materials and resources
  • Complimentary refreshments (for premium tickets)
  • Exclusive access to certain areas (for VIP tickets)

Our refund policy is as follows:

  • Full refund up to 7 days before the event
  • 50% refund up to 3 days before the event
  • No refunds within 3 days of the event

Special circumstances may be considered on a case-by-case basis.

We welcome volunteers! Here's how you can get involved:

  • Fill out our volunteer application form
  • Attend a volunteer orientation session
  • Choose your preferred event and role
  • Receive training and event details
